About Us

Little Pont is the first interactive role play center to open in the local area, run by dedicated instructors. A child-sized town, modelled upon the actual village of Ponteland, means that the businesses and buildings are familiar to the children. Here, children can learn about the different business in their communities, and how theys help to keep the our town vibrant! Role play environments promote hands-on experiential learning for children to learn how people live and work together. Children will have the opportunity to role play different occupations and practice everyday life skills in a little town that they recognise!

Find Us

We are located in the centre of Ponteland, between the bank and the barbers. Follow sat nav directions for NE209QA and you will find us. Parking is located behind the premises and is well signposted.

We have a passion for teaching children so we have integrated inspiring and interactive STEM workshops into each session. Each session is age-specific and so we are able to keep children engaged by creating age-appropriate workshops. And most importantly, it's so much fun!

Party Bookings

Our Party Bookings are available to book on Saturdays. They run for 2 hours and include fun STEM activities, as well as full access to the role play area. Party packages can be taylored to you and your childs wishes. Get in touch to book a fantastic party for your little one and their friends!

Book a Course

Come and join us! Our sessions run Monday to Thursday and are age-specific. Bookings are as a block of 4 weeks at one session per week. We offer sessions for Babies (0 to 1 year), Toddlers (1 to 2.5 years), Preschool (2.5 to 4 years) and Early years (4 to 5 years).
