Baby Bells

Our baby group is suitable for babies from birth up to 12 months. The sessions aim to ignite and delight our little ones to new experiences in our magical baby area.

Connection

In a world of 'busy', this class is great for providing a calm space to just 'be' with your baby. Time to connect with your baby and meeting new friends

Sensory

Gentle sensorial play is a key focus of the session. Held in our indoor 'baby garden', twinkling lights, beautiful colours and nature sounds are used to nourish those newly developing senses without being overstimulating

Development

Your baby's brain is miraculous, each experience forms millions of new connections and supports growth and development. Our teachers Katie and Janine teach new and exciting STEM sessions within each class to help your baby explore the world in new ways!
