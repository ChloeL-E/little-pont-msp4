Preschool Class

Our Preschool course is suitable for children aged 3 to 4 years.

Connection

Your child developes conversation skills by role-modelling, prompting, guiding and practising. These skills help childrens' development and relationships. Little Pont is set up to encourage communication within the groups. Little Pont facilitates this learning through building of communication skills through imaginary play. Children of this age often begin to enjoy playing alongside each other and collaborating in role play scenarios.

Creativity

Preschoolers learn and develop through creative activities like drama and role play. Experiencing, discovering and experimenting are the most important parts of preschooler creative activities. The exciting STEM session within each class is designed to engage each child in the group and ignite their enjoyment of Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths from an early age.

Development

At Little Pont we understand that play is key to cognitive, motor skills, emotional and language development. Our aim is to facilitate the development of all these skills in a fun environment, supporting them in their readiness to start school.

Course Details

Courses run in 4 week blocks so you will get 4 classes per booking. Each of our age groups run every day Monday - Thursday. The times differ to give you choice over the most suitable time for you and your child. Each class runs for 90 minutes from the start time, so there is plenty of time for your litte one to enjoy themselves.

Check out our courses below to find the best day and time for you! Once you have chosen your child's course, click the button and you will be asked to complete some details in order to set up your account before proceeding to payment. We look forward to welcoming you and your child to Little Pont. If you have any problems or if you wish to send us a booking enquiry don't hesitate to get in touch .
